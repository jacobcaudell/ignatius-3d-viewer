Who we are

Caudell Spatial Intelligence produces high-fidelity 3D models of terrain and structures. Our work combines advanced photogrammetry with modern computer vision to deliver digital assets that are accurate, reliable, and visually compelling.

What we deliver

  • Precision terrain & structures — Dense 3D reconstructions, digital elevation models, and CAD/GIS-ready layers.
  • Analytic insight — Breakdowns of surfaces, slopes, and geometry that support design and decision-making.
  • Cinematic 3D — NeRF training and interactive models for communication, visualization, and archives.

Why trust us

  • Founded by a PhD in Mathematics with proven research in geometry, statistical modeling, and machine learning.
  • FAA certified commercial drone pilot with over a decade of experience producing as-built models and documentation for large-scale construction projects.
  • Proven workflows that combine scientific rigor with field-hardened experience in large-scale modeling.
  • Deliverables optimized for design professionals, institutions, and organizations that demand clarity and accuracy.
