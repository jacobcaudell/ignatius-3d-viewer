Pondside Green Gasson Hall St. Ignatius

Large-scale terrain modeling requires systematic capture, scale, and comprehensive analysis. This project demonstrates expertise in environmental modeling, combining systematic drone capture with ground control to deliver high-fidelity terrain models. When you need to understand every contour, slope, and feature across hundreds of acres, photogrammetry provides the detail that traditional methods often miss.

Terrain Analysis & Visualization

Pondside Green: Altitude Heatmap & 3D Flythrough

Project Scope: 200+ acres of terrain modeling for golf course redesign and construction planning
Capture Method: Systematic grid pattern at 250 ft AGL using professional drone
Ground Control: 20 control points with precision positioning and corrections
Outputs: High-resolution point clouds, meshes, and decision-grade terrain models for design and construction
