Pondside Green Gasson Hall St. Ignatius

Interactive 3D visualization transforms static documentation into immersive experiences. This project demonstrates how cutting-edge 3D technology can bring art to life, allowing viewers to explore Pablo Eduardo's 2005 sculpture St. Ignatius of Loyola from any angle in real-time. Experience the intersection of artistic interpretation and technological innovation through dual visual styles that showcase the versatility of modern 3D reconstruction.
