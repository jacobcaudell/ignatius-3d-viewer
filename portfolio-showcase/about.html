Background

I work with spatial data and 3D visualization, combining mathematical approaches with practical applications. My background includes work on complex spatial modeling projects and research in applied mathematics, with a focus on photogrammetry, computer vision, and mathematical rigor. With a decade of experience producing as-builts on complex construction sites, I understand how costly bad information can be and how transformative clarity is.

My approach blends advanced photogrammetry, computer vision, and mathematical rigor to deliver models that are high-quality, reliable, visually compelling, and ready when the stakes are high. I focus on creating accurate, useful representations of real-world spaces and structures through professional drone operations, high-performance computing workflows, and cutting-edge visualization technologies.
